About Us

Nycil Limited

Nycil Limited incorporated in 1978 has remained active in the manufacturing of over 150 types of resins and chemicals. As a foremost resin producer, we are diversified in the chemical and allied products for other industries.
Nycil has been rated high and remain the market leader as the largest manufacturer of Synthetic Resins and allied products in the chemical world in Nigeria. This we owe to our manufacturing standards, quality measures and a continual research development of our products from inception.

We produce high purity resin. We build esteemed products and are committed to customers satisfaction, propelled by excellent production through research and Development proficiency and a dedicated workforce.

Mission Statement

To manufacture and market high quality products and services driven by technological innovations, research and development in order to meet the expectations of our customers

Vision Statement

To remain the clear, unassailable leader in the synthetic resin and allied chemical industry.

Quality Policy

Nycil limited is committed to producing and improving high quality synthetic resins, emulsions and allied chemicals as well as providing technical support to satisfy the needs of our valued customers.

It is our commitment to also ensure the effective implementation of Quality Management System by satisfying applicable requirements, addressing the context of our organization and continually improving our Quality Management system. The Top Management of Nycil ensures that quality objectives at the corporate and Departmental levels are adequately established to achieve our Quality Policy.

Quality Objectives

In order to achieve Nycil Limited Quality Policy and consistently satisfy customer expectations and all applicable requirements, the quality objectives of Nycil are:

  • To ensure efficient operation of the production plants by reducing production delays and losses to the barest minimum practicable (5%).
  • To ensure that there is an efficient mechanism for importing specified goods of right quality and best price such that 90% of products are delivered according to specification within the year.
  • To ensure achievement of 80% of the sales budget To achieve at least 90% customer satisfaction.
