Welcome to Nycil Limited

We have been rated high and remain the market leader as the largest manufacturer of Synthetic Resins and allied products in the chemical world in Nigeria. This we owe to our manufacturing standards, quality measures and a continuous research development of our products from inception.

We are unmatched by others in the manufacturing of chemicals for Paints, Textile, Adhesive, Packaging, Oil & Gas, foam chemicals and chemicals for other diverse sectors, just to mention a few.
